## Description (4000 characters max)

**IMCircle — The social network for people who grow.**

IMCircle is a growth-focused social network built for students, creators, founders, professionals, and freelancers who want to build, learn, share their journey, find opportunities, and grow with the right circle.

Most social apps are built for consumption. IMCircle is built for growth. Every feature — your Circle, your Journey, your Builder Score, your Learning feed — exists to help you find the right people around you and make consistent, visible progress with them.

WHAT YOU CAN DO ON IMCIRCLE
• Share your journey — post real, ongoing progress on what you're building, learning, or working toward, and let people follow along milestone by milestone.
• Learn in public — post bite-sized learning updates and see what others in your circle are learning right now.
• Build your Circle — connect with builders, students, founders, creators, and professionals who are on a similar path, instead of a generic follower list.
• Track your growth — a Builder Score and streaks reflect real, consistent activity, not vanity likes.
• Find opportunities — discover openings and connect with people building things worth joining.
• Join communities — Circles are focused spaces around a shared interest where you can post, discuss, and grow together.

FOUNDER STORY
IMCircle was founded by Rishu Raj, who came from a small-town/village background and moved to Delhi with big dreams. He believes a person's future is shaped by the people around them — and that many ambitious young people across Bharat never get access to the right environment, network, or exposure to reach their potential. IMCircle exists to close that gap: a digital circle where they can find builders, learners, creators, founders, opportunities, and inspiration, wherever they're starting from.

WHO IT'S FOR
Students figuring out what to build or learn next, first-generation and student founders, creators documenting their process, freelancers and early-career professionals building a track record — and anyone who wants their network to be about where they're going, not just where they've been.

SAFETY AND CONTROL
Report and block are available on every profile and post, account deletion is a few taps away in Settings, and our Community Guidelines explain what's expected from everyone on the platform.

Find your circle. Grow together.

## App Store review notes (for the "Notes" field in App Store Connect)
IMCircle is a social network for students, creators, founders, professionals, and freelancers to share their growth journey, learn in public, and find the right community. Account creation requires phone/email verification (OTP) or Google Sign-In.

To review core flows:
1. Sign up (or use the demo credentials provided separately in the App Review notes field/attachment) and complete profile setup.
2. View the Home feed, open a Journey, and open the Learning feed.
3. Reporting: open any post or profile → "⋯" → Report.
4. Blocking: Settings → Blocked Accounts, or block directly from a profile's "⋯" menu.
5. Account deletion: Settings → Account → Delete Account (type-to-confirm).

Camera/Photo Library permission prompts appear only when a user chooses "Take Photo" or "Photo Library" while creating a post, updating their avatar, or adding a journey/learning update — this is standard WKWebView file-input behavior, not a background permission request.

No third-party ad SDKs or tracking SDKs are integrated. Third-party data processors used: Google (Sign-In, Maps), Cloudinary (media hosting), MSG91 (SMS OTP delivery) — disclosed in the Privacy Policy.
