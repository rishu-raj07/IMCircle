## Full description (4000 characters max)

**IMCircle — The social network for people who grow.**

IMCircle is a growth-focused social network built for students, creators, founders, professionals, and freelancers who want to build, learn, share their journey, find opportunities, and grow with the right circle.

Most social apps are built for consumption. IMCircle is built for growth. Every feature — your Circle, your Journey, your Builder Score, your Learning feed — exists to help you find the right people around you and make consistent, visible progress with them.

**What you can do on IMCircle:**
- Share your journey — post real, ongoing progress on what you're building, learning, or working toward, and let people follow along milestone by milestone.
- Learn in public — post bite-sized learning updates and see what others in your circle are learning right now.
- Build your Circle — connect with builders, students, founders, creators, and professionals who are on a similar path, instead of a generic follower list.
- Track your growth — a Builder Score and streaks reflect real, consistent activity, not vanity likes.
- Find opportunities — discover openings and connect with people building things worth joining.
- Join communities — Circles are focused spaces around a shared interest (a startup idea, a skill, a career path) where you can post, discuss, and grow together.

**Founder story**

IMCircle was founded by Rishu Raj, who came from a small-town/village background and moved to Delhi with big dreams. He believes a person's future is shaped by the people around them — and that many ambitious young people across Bharat never get access to the right environment, network, or exposure to reach their potential. IMCircle exists to close that gap: a digital circle where they can find builders, learners, creators, founders, opportunities, and inspiration, wherever they're starting from.

**Who IMCircle is for**
- Students figuring out what to build or learn next
- First-generation founders and student founders
- Creators documenting their process publicly
- Freelancers and early-career professionals building a track record
- Anyone who wants their network to be about where they're going, not just where they've been

**Safety and control**
IMCircle includes reporting and blocking on every profile and post, a clear account-deletion flow in Settings, and Community Guidelines that explain what's expected from everyone on the platform. See our Privacy Policy and Community Guidelines from within the app or at imcircle.com.

## Data safety section notes (Play Console → App content → Data safety)
Declare collection of: account info (name, email/phone, profile photo), user-generated content (posts, journey updates, comments, messages), and usage/analytics data (in-app events for product analytics). Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS only). Users can request account and data deletion in-app (Settings → Account → Delete Account). Third-party processors to disclose by name: Google (Sign-In, Maps), Cloudinary (media hosting/CDN), MSG91 (SMS OTP delivery). No data is sold to third parties. No advertising ID / ad-tracking SDKs are integrated.

## Content moderation notes (for Play Console review notes)
Users can report any post, comment, or profile via the "⋯" menu, and can block any user from their profile or from Settings → Blocked Accounts, which immediately hides that user's content and prevents contact both ways. Reports go to the IMCircle team (not the reported user) for review; the Community Guidelines page explains what happens after a report. There is no anonymous public posting — all content is tied to an authenticated account.

## Account deletion notes
Users can permanently delete their account from Settings → Account → Delete Account (type-to-confirm flow). This is also documented in the Privacy Policy and Community Guidelines, satisfying Play Console's account-deletion requirement (a working in-app path, not just a web form).
